Casino Craps

Instructions:

To Win: Roll a 7 or 11 on the first roll.
To Lose: Roll a 12, 2, or 3 on the first roll.

If you roll a 1, 4, 5, 6, or 8, that number becomes your points and you get to roll again.
After the first roll, if you roll a 7 you lose. You must now roll the same number twice in a row to win.
